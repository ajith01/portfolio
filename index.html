01.About Me

Hi, My name is Ajith Kusalakumar. I started programming while I was studying Mathematics and Physics at the University of Toronto. I learned python to analyze data gathered in experiments and later learned C++ to program microcontrollers for these experiments. I enjoyed programming a lot more than physics experiments.

Once I graduated, I enrolled into the Computer Science program at Algoma University. I got quite a bit of transfer credits, which allowed me to complete the degree at an accelerated pace.

I had gotten a research assistant position in the computer science department where I worked on multiple projects throughout my studies in the areas of encryption, networking, and machine learning.

I was accepted into the master's program of my choice, but I realized that I enjoyed writing software over research, so I declined my offer to find a job I love.

That leads us to today. I am currently focusing on web development.

I am looking for an entry-level software engineering job.
